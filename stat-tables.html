Stat & Equipment Tables

Numerical source of truth for all equipment, modifier, and progression values · validated against the damage formula at levels 1 / 25 / 50 / 75 / 100 · includes acquisition & upgrade economy · July 2026

0 — Core formulas (revised)

Creature stat (Atk/Def):  Stat = (Core + Σ TagStats) × AxisMult × QM
                              Core = 1.6 × (level + 25)     AxisMult = (3 × axisGrade + 5) / 20
                              (identical naked values to the old (g×30+50) curve — pure reframe)
Creature Speed (flat):             Speed    = axisGrade          (1-10, equals axis grade)
Creature HP:                       HP       = (4 × level + 35) × (5 + DefenseGrade) / 11
Creature quality multiplier:       QM       = 0.80 + 0.04 × qualityGrade      (applies to Atk/Def/HP — NEVER Speed)

Character Attack:   Σ ItemStats(Attack)  × ClassAxisMult      ClassAxisMult = (3 × stageStat + 5) / 20
Character Defense:  Σ ItemStats(Defense) × ClassAxisMult      (no innate pool — gear is everything)
Character HP:       (4 × level + 35) × (5 + stageDefStat) / 11 + Σ flat HP items
Character Speed:    15 (flat, no gear sources — only in-battle stat stages)

Damage:  RawDamage   = ((2 × Level / 5 + 2) × MovePower × Attack / Defense) / 130 + 2 + Attack / 50
         FinalDamage = RawDamage × Synergy/Anti × Random(0.9–1.1) × (1 + Σ increased%) × Π(more)
         No same-type damage bonus and no type-effectiveness multiplier — there is no type chart.
         Type interaction lives only in each move's Synergy and Anti clauses.
The earlier Battle System formula (g×6+2)×L/100+g×3 was inconsistent with its own level-100 table; the revised base-stat formula above reproduces the committed table exactly (grade×30+50 at level 100) and keeps grade ratios level-invariant. HP was re-curved linear-in-level so turns-to-kill stays constant while leveling.

Stat aggregation (multiplier model). ItemStat = (implicit + Σ gems) × (1 + Σ rune %) × (1 + Σ Local enchant %) × refinement, flat enchants add into the base band. Character: Σ ItemStats × ClassAxisMult where ClassAxisMult = (3 × stageStat + 5) / 20 — Paladin Def 10 → ×1.75; the character has no innate pool. 7 — Creature quality grade multiplier

QM = 0.80 + 0.04 × qualityGrade — multiplies Attack, Defense, and HP. Never Speed (flat speed tiers are a design pillar). Wild eggs cap at Legendary; Abyssal–Void come from breeding only.

Void vs Worn = ×1.20 / ×0.84 = 43% more stats at identical species, level, and gear.

8 — Character stats per level (multiplier model)

Characters have no innate stat pool. CharStat = Σ ItemStats × ClassAxisMult, ClassAxisMult = (3 × stageStat + 5) / 20 (stat 1 ×0.40 · stat 5 ×1.00 · stat 10 ×1.75). HP = (4 × level + 35) × (5 + stageDefStat) / 11 + Σ flat HP items · Speed = 15 flat.

An HP-stacked creature wall (all-Vitality tag runes, trading away Aegis) reaches 1,879 HP / 19.3 turns vs a maxed striker — the deliberate wall premium. Ward (+Defense accessory) was retired on max-build math: swapping a maxed Relic (+401 HP) for a maxed Ward (+647 Defense) drops a wall from 14.4 to 10.7 turns — the Attack/50 floor is Defense-immune and the small HP pool makes flat HP marginally stronger; Defense investment on choice slots cannot compete. The + Attack/50 damage floor is what holds maxed mirrors inside the band — without it these fights ran 58–74 turns. Progression rows are a spam-only, synergy-free floor; real rotations (Heavy/Nuke cycling, Synergy clauses, slight over-leveling) run ~40–60% faster, landing PvE trash at 3–5 and boss/wall fights at 8–12.

13 — Refinement (+1 to +10): success & cost

No pity — every attempt is a pure roll. Failure consumes Scrap only; the item is never destroyed or downgraded. Attempt cost = 3 × target level × item tier Scrap.

Expected 0→+10 at T10: 158 attempts, ~43,700 Scrap ≈ 290 h at endgame income (~150 Scrap/h). The +10 gate alone: lucky (p10) 10 attempts, median 69, unlucky (p90) 229. Failure costs currency only — no item destruction, no downgrade — which is what justifies the low raw odds at the top of the ladder.
