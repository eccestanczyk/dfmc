WIN STREAK & FLOORS

Other Systems › Win Streak & Floors

The tower is 100 floors — 10 zones of 10. Each floor is cleared by winning a streak of consecutive battles; a single loss resets the streak to zero. Difficulty pressure comes from that reset, not from resource attrition.

Clear condition

  • Win a streak of consecutive battles on a floor to open the portal to the next floor. Threshold: ceil(floor^0.65).
  • Losing a battle resets the streak to zero. You are not removed from the floor — the team stays put and rebuilds. No forced exit, no floor lock, no return to floor-select.
  • Boss floors (every 10th) always require exactly 1 battle — the boss fight itself. No wild encounters on boss floors.
  • Full HP is restored after every battle, win or lose. Healing items were removed entirely; attrition exists only within a single battle.

Boss floors (10, 20, … 100) override the formula: always 1 battle.

Streak counter

After every battle a compact rectangle shows X / Y (wins / threshold). On a win, X scrolls up and the text pops briefly; on a loss, the rectangle cracks and X rolls back down to 0. It only appears on floors not yet cleared.

Autoplay behaviour

Auto-advance floors

When the threshold is met and the portal opens, advance automatically. Off → keep farming the current floor. Autoplay never auto-enters boss rooms.

On death

Stay — remain on the floor and rebuild the streak. Go down — drop one floor and continue there.

Wild creature levels scale with floor (floor ±3, bell-distributed), and floor equals level — so the streak threshold and the enemy levels both climb together as you ascend. Which evolutionary stage appears in the wild is governed per line by evolution levels, not by floor bands.
