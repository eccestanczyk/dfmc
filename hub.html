The Tower Awaits

A dark-fantasy, creature-collecting, competitive turn-based RPG. In this prototype: climb 100 floors; hatch, breed, and evolve 300 monsters; upgrade equipment; and tune your builds to reach the top of the leaderboards. Auto-mode takes the grind out of leveling — but to compete, you sharpen your own strategies and outplay other players head-to-head.
