GRADE MULTIPLIER

Other Systems › Grade Multiplier

Every creature has a quality grade from Worn to Void. Grade applies one shared multiplier to a creature's stats — identical across every species — so grade is a clean, universal power axis independent of type or role.

The multiplier

Base stats come from axis grade + level. That base is then scaled by the quality multiplier:

QM = 0.80 + 0.04 × qualityGrade

  • Applies to Offense, Defense, Utility, and HP.
  • Never applies to Speed — Speed equals axis grade flat (1–10), with no quality, level, or gear scaling.
  • Grade is rolled at hatch, weighted by egg grade (up to 4 grades above the egg, any grade below — see the Eggs codex), and is the same for all species: a Void creature and a Worn creature of the same axis grades differ only by this multiplier.

Range spans ×0.84 (Worn) to ×1.20 (Void) — a 43% spread from floor to ceiling. Epic (grade 5) is the ×1.00 anchor.

Grade is one of two independent gear/creature power axes. It differs from equipment refinement (+1…+10) and from axis grade (which shapes a creature's stat distribution). Full numerical stat tables live in the Stat & Equipment Tables.
