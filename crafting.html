CRAFTING

Nothing is useless. Every drop dismantles into materials. Every material crafts the next tier.

Recipes are authored, not formulaic. Every craftable item has its own row in codex/recipes.csv with an explicit material bill. There is no cost formula anywhere in the crafting system — change a recipe by editing its row. Output grade is always Worn; grading up is the separate Ichor system on the Upgrading page.

Gear combining

3 × same-slot, same-grade gear → 1 random base type of that slot at the next grade. Capped at Epic. Legendary and above come from Ichor gambles and lucky drops only.

Example

3 × Rare Longsword → 1 × Epic (random weapon base)
Epic is grade 5 — the market entry tier. Deterministically grindable.
