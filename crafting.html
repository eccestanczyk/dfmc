Gear combining

3 × same-slot, same-grade gear → 1 random base type of that slot at the next grade. Capped at Epic. Legendary and above come from Ichor gambles and lucky drops only.

Example

3 × Rare Blade → 1 × Epic (random weapon base)
Epic is grade 5 — the market entry tier. Deterministically grindable.
