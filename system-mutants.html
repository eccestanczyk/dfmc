MUTANT VARIANTS

Other Systems › Mutant Variants

Any egg can hatch as a Mutant — a creature with an alternate colour scheme from a rare pigmentation mutation. It is purely cosmetic: a medal on the creature sheet and a recoloured palette, with no effect on stats or gameplay.

Hatch chance by egg grade

Chance scales with egg grade. Completing the Codex adds a permanent flat +0.1% to every mutant hatch chance.

Codex completion tiers

The Codex bonus is not one all-or-nothing unlock — it is earned zone by zone. Each of the ten zones has its own Codex tier: Acquire (hatch) every creature that appears on that zone's floors and the tier locks in permanently.

Each zone tier grants a permanent +0.01% flat Mutant hatch chance. All ten zones = the full Codex = +0.10%, exactly the completion bonus in the table above — the reward was always +0.1%; it is now paid out in ten instalments instead of one.

Zones overlap by design. Creatures appear on floors across several zones (a creature's Floor_Found reads like 1;11;21;32), so each zone's set is 90 creatures and the ten sets union to all 300. A creature counts toward every zone tier whose floors it appears on — completing the Grasslands tier therefore also carries you part-way through the tiers above it, and the ten tiers finish together only when the last creature is Acquired.

Acquired means hatched. Seeing a creature in the wild marks it Seen in the Codex but does not count toward a tier.

How it behaves

  • Cosmetic only — grants a medal and a recoloured palette; identical stats, moves, and grade to a normal hatch.
  • Codex completion bonus — a permanent +0.1% added to all grades at full Codex, accrued +0.01% per completed zone tier; at full completion it roughly doubles the low end and lifts the ceiling to 0.20%.
  • Fusion carries it forward — fusing two creatures combines their XP and keeps the higher grade; the result is a Mutant if either parent was one.
Even at the Void ceiling the base rate is 0.10% (1 in 1,000), so Mutants stay a genuine rarity flex. Because fusion passes the trait on, a single Mutant hatch can seed a lineage of recoloured fusions.
