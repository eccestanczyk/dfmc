DROPTABLES

How Drops Work

Every wild creature defeated makes three independent rolls — one for gear, one for an enhancement (gem / rune / scroll, even split), and one for the defeated line's Stage-1 egg. Each roll uses the same per-floor chance table below. When a gear roll succeeds, the base is a uniform pick among the gear bases (weapons, armour, accessories, tags); a successful enhancement roll splits evenly across gem / rune / scroll, uniform within the family.

Per-kill chances are scaled on the game-wide rarity value ladder — C 1.0 / UC 1.5 / R 2.5 / SR 4.0 / SSR 7.0 (the same ladder as XP and Food) — and normalized per floor against codex/encounter_weights.csv, so each drop family lands exactly 10% per battle (3 kills) in expectation on every floor of the tower. Rarer kills pay better; the floor-wide economy never drifts.

All chances on this page are read live from the codex CSVs — drop_chances_by_floor.csv, drop_grade_weights.csv, key_drop_rates.csv, boss_loot.csv, first_win_drops.csv — the same tables the game reads. Nothing is hardcoded.

Drop Grade Weights by Floor Band

When a gear drop lands, its grade is rolled from the band of the floor it dropped on. Divine, Primordial, and Void never drop — the Ichor gamble is the only path to Divine+ gear, and breeding the only path to Divine+ creatures.

Band shifts: Enhancements (gems, runes, scrolls) roll their grade one band behind gear. Boss gear rolls one band ahead of the boss's band (the final band clamps to itself). Wild and boss eggs use the floor band clamped to the Legendary wild-egg cap.

Boss Keys

A single zone-specific Key (ITM-KEY-010…ITM-KEY-100) is required to enter a boss floor and is consumed on entry. Every wild creature defeated on the zone's 9 preceding floors rolls for that zone's Key, per creature, scaled on the rarity value ladder and normalized so a minimum-win journey through the zone yields exactly one Key in expectation.

Guaranteed key: the first time a character clears the 9th floor of a zone (floors 9, 19, … 99), a Key is guaranteed to drop — one-time per zone per character.

Boss Loot

Every boss kill guarantees two drops, delivered on the battle-conclusion screen straight to inventory:

One piece of equipment — a uniform pick among the Weapon / Armour / Accessory bases, at the boss floor's tier, with grade rolled one band ahead of the boss's floor band (the final band clamps to itself). Divine+ never drops.

One native egg — a Stage-1 egg of a line native to the zone (Home_Zone), weighted by the canonical rarity chances 30/30/20/10/10 (C/UC/R/SR/SSR) normalized over the tiers present in the zone. Grade from the boss's floor band, clamped to the Legendary wild-egg cap — breeding remains the only path to Divine+ and Void creatures.

Source: codex/boss_loot.csv

First-Win Bonus Drops

The first 10 wild-battle wins each grant an additional guaranteed drop on top of the normal rolls. All grants spawn at Worn, Tier 1. Boss wins do not advance the counter. Win 1 rolls the accessory family at random; win 4 grants the complement. Egg grants (wins 1 and 9) give the Stage-1 egg of the lowest-rarity enemy present, random among ties.
