EGGS

Hatching Mechanics

Eggs are consumable items that hatch into creatures. Every egg is bound to a creature type — its shell shows which — and hatching one always produces a creature whose first type matches the egg. A Beast Egg hatches a Beast; a Void Egg hatches something worse.

Every egg also drops at a grade, using the same five grades as creatures: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Super Rare, and Super Super Rare. The egg's grade does not fix the hatchling's grade — instead, each grade of egg has a chance to spawn a creature of each grade. Higher-grade eggs simply weight the roll toward higher-grade creatures.

Gender

Every hatchling also rolls a gender at a flat 50/50 — male and female are equally likely from any egg, of any type or grade. The result is permanent and is shown as a sign immediately after the creature's name wherever it appears: ♂ for male, ♀ for female. See Gender for the full rules.
