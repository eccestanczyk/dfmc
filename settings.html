Hub › Settings

The in-game Settings menu. One full-screen panel with a section rail on the left and options on the right (mobile: sections collapse into an accordion). Every option persists locally and syncs with cloud save. Defaults are chosen for a first-time mobile player.

Layout

  • Opened from the gear icon on the main HUD. Full-screen overlay, dark parchment-and-iron chrome consistent with the codex aesthetic.
  • Section rail (left, Cinzel labels): Audio · Display · Battle & Autoplay · Adventure Log · Notifications · Account & Data · Language & Accessibility · Legal & Support.
  • Control types: slider (0–100), toggle, segmented (2–4 exclusive choices), dropdown, action button (opens dialog/page).
  • Footer bar: Restore Defaults (per open section, with confirm) and game version/build string.
  • Changes apply instantly — no Save button. Destructive actions always confirm.
