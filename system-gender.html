GENDER

Other Systems › Gender

Every creature has a gender, rolled at the moment it hatches. The roll is a flat 50/50 — male and female are equally likely for every creature, every egg, every grade.

The roll

  • Fixed 50/50 — gender is decided on hatch and is never re-rolled.
  • Unweighted — egg grade, egg type, creature grade, and creature line have no effect on the odds.
  • Permanent — gender does not change on level-up or evolution; a creature carries the same gender across all three stages of its line.

Display

Gender is shown as a sign immediately after the creature's name, everywhere the name appears — party list, battle UI, creature sheet, and reliquary.
