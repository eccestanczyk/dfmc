▶ 💎 Enhancements Gems · Runes · Scrolls
💎 Gems — Flat Stats

Fixed flat modifiers (+X Offense, +X Defense, +X HP). Up to 3 slots at Void grade. Dismantles into Dust.

🔷 Runes — Percentage Scaling

Percentage modifiers (+X% Offense, +X% Defense). Multiply base + gems. Up to 3 slots at Void. Dismantles into Essence.

📜 Scrolls — Enchantments

On socket, rolls a random modifier from pool. Rerollable for Ink. Up to 3 slots at Void. Dismantles into Ink.
