UPGRADING

No pity. No failstacks. No protection. Every attempt is a pure roll. Failure costs currency only — items are never destroyed or downgraded.

Refinement (+1 to +10)

A more multiplier. On character gear (Local scope) it multiplies that item's total contribution. On Tags (Personal scope) it multiplies the creature's totals from that tag. Currency: Scrap. Cost per attempt: 3 × target level × item tier.

Soul grading (Worn → Void)

Class souls only — grading applies to souls slotted into the character's eight gear slots; creature-side souls do not grade. A class soul's grade is independent of the source creature's quality grade and is retained through the source line's evolution. All souls start Worn. SoulMult = 1 + 0.05 × (grade# − 1) multiplies the soul's output (passive magnitude / granted move power) — Worn ×1.00 → Void ×1.45. Each attempt costs mult × 5 units, payable in any combination of the five sink currencies at their income-rate weights: 1 unit = 15 Scrap = 1 Ichor = 2 Dust = 2 Essence = 2 Ink (each ≈ equal farm time). Same success ladder as gear. Food excluded (breeding-only economy).
