DEATH & LEAGUES

Other Systems › Death & Leagues

Every account holds one character in every unlocked league, each starting at level 1. The character slot is permanent — death never removes or moves it. What death costs depends on the league.

The four leagues

Eternal

Soft death: stay on the floor, streak resets, character unchanged. Houses the Eternal crypt and receives contents migrated from seasonal leagues.

Hardcore

On death the character is wiped to level 1 and stays in Hardcore. All items, creatures, and class XP (as Soul Gems) go to the Eternal crypt.

Seasonal

Soft death like Eternal. Timed league; at season end the character migrates to Eternal and the Seasonal crypt is delivered to the Eternal crypt.

HC Seasonal

On death, wiped to level 1 in place; items go to the Seasonal crypt. At season end the HC Seasonal crypt is delivered to the Hardcore crypt.

Soul Gems

When a hardcore character is wiped, one Soul Gem is generated per class they had any XP in, deposited in the crypt. Rank scales with the XP amount. Consuming a gem grants that XP to the surviving character's matching class — softening a wipe without erasing the loss.

One character at a time

  • The game simulates one league character at a time. Switching leagues switches the active character and pauses the previous one entirely.
  • There is no offline progression — being in-game is the only way to advance. Economy is balanced in battles for this one active character (~30 battles/hour on autoplay).
  • The character slot is permanent in every league; even a hardcore wipe leaves the character in place at level 1.
Hardcore stakes scale with the leveling curve: a relevel to 67 is ~30 h, but a death at level 95+ costs weeks. See XP & Leveling for the full curve. In-battle defeat (not permadeath) is covered under Win Streak & Floors.
